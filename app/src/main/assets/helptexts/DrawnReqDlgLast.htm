ExaustiveDraw Claim is opend to all players when no one called "Win" for last tile discarded.
You can re-open from menu:Draw.
You can claim Tenpai and NagashiMangan.
After all players Send, ExaustiveDraw Confirmation is opened on the dealer. The hand of the player claimed Tenpai is opened on board, the hand of player claimed "Riichi" and "Tenpai" are displayed on the dialog.
By dealer's Send on ExaustiveDraw Confirmation, ExaustiveDraw Resposne is opened on all clients. When all clients responsed OK, ExaustiveDraw completes.

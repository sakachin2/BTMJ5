Rules about Yaku(define pattern of winning hand and its value).
To check whethere winning hand is complete or not, set "Yes" on topic Checking winning hand of Oper Setting. Even if "Yes" is set, any wining hand by local rule can be claimed by menu:Win anyway.
After you called "Win", open Win Claim dialog by Score button and claim your hand's value by count of han and fu. "Win Claim" is sent to the other players, after all player responsed OK or NG, End-of-Hand dialog opens on the dealer which displays winning tiles.
If the dealer is Robot, the dialog opens on the next(counter clockwise) human player. The dealer checks validity of the win call, then send the dialog to the other player. After receiving "OK" from the other players, Fix the game to complete "End-of-Hand".
If dealer found "Chombo"("Furiten", "Noten", "Noten Riichi", "Constraint by han" etc), dealer can set Invalid(without penalty), or Chombo(with penalty). In that case dealer can select "Gameover" or "Continue" with "DeadHand" of "Chombo" players. DeadHand player just discard drawn tile and can not do any call.

KeiTen 1-han
At exaustiveDraw, if your-hand is not status of Tenpai(waiting winning tile) with Yaku, you have to pay panalty of NoTen(Not Tenpai). This allows to assume some format of hands as Tenpai .
None for all:not allow any KeiTen.
Empty OK:Empty means all winning tiles are exausted on river, earth and in your hands.
Furiten OK:Allows winning tiles are Furiten status such as you discarded one of winning tile.
0-han OK:Yaku is not requred by any winning tile.
2-han constrant
Under 2-han constraint condition, 0-han OK:no Yaku is required. 2-han possibility:requres possibility of Yaku more than 2-han by a winning tile. But if KeiTen 1-han is "0-han OK", requires 1-han possibility.
Fix Yaku
Japanese style Mahjong requires your winning hand to have some Yaku(at least 1 han). Some rule prohibit Ron by multiple tiles wait. e.g. When you opened some melds and are waiting pair of dragon(1 han) and number tiles, you can not call Ron for the dragon tile discarded. If you are waiting pair of two type of dragon tiles, you can call win for either one of two.
It may exist some variation for "First only", make agreement among players beforehand.
Riichi For Robot game:Select from "Disable OpenRiichi", "Ignore Robot's discard" or "Pay normal(Robot pays the normal score with 1-han of "Open" added).

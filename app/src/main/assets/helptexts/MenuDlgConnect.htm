Support of multiple connection depends on the device. If it is not supported, game including 2 robots is available by Settings-Robot.
It seems to me that Bluetooth is easy to use. If it is troublesome, try Wifi Direct. Both connection do not exist simultaneously, disconnect all when switches connection type. "Discover"ing neighbor process takes some time at first in both case.

Bluetooth：This App supports only legacy mode Bluetooth. Device supporting only BLE(Low Enagy) mode is not available, try "Wifi Direct". Radio-frequency interference may occur if the device has telephone function. If the device is dual-band(2.4GHz and 5GHz), assign 5GHz to telephone. Bluetooth uses 2.4GHz.

Wifi Direct：It may be more power consuming than Bluetooth. Transmission delay may occur caused by base protocol. (Detecting connection failure may take considarable time).
From Android6.0(MarchMallow), you may be asked to give permission to use Wifi Direct.

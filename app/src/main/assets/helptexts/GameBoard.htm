Buttons 1.
Rule : Open "RuleSettings" dialog. Changing rule is not available in this situation.
Score : Open "Win Claim" dialog to claim value of your win.
At win called, highlighten on the device called win. For Robot's win, it is 1st human player starting from "East". Push again when you closed the dialog accidentally.
Riichi : Declare Riichi. Press after selected tile to discard. When pushed it changes to Can.Ri to allow cancel Riichi.
By Confirm Tenpai option of Oper setting, your selected tile to Discard may cause error msg "Not Tenpai". Re-select valid tile to discard then retry Riichi button.
When Confirm Tenpai is off, the Riichi may be Chombo of Noten Riichi error.
OpenRi : Declare Open-Riichi. Press before "Discard". When pushed it changes to Can.OR to accept cancel Open-Riichi. This button is visible if Allow Open of RuleSettings,Yaku,Riichi is checked on.

Buttons 2.
Win, Call(Pon, Chii, Kan), Draw have periods for action each at RuleSettings,Oper,DelayTime.
This is for, e.g. protecting anyone to claim Win after you called Pon that decrease his chance of win.
Draw : You can also draw by touching small green circle on wall. This button's visibility is option of Preference.
When playing alone, setting option allow to you to force to draw not by timeout but Draw button touch.
Discard : Tile just drawn or touched is framed green and candidate of discard. Flicking the tile is also "Discard"ing. This button's visibility is option of Preference.
Cancel : By RuleSettings.Oper.Wait!:win call is cancelable, this button appears when Win was pressed.
In this situation, Push "Win" again to declare Win, push "Cancel" to cancel "Win".
For call Pon,Chii and Draw+Kan, when selecting tile is required for multiple Melds candidate. Recall after selecting tile for a meld completes the call. You can not cancel the call. It is canceled by timeout if set.
When playing alone with notify mode, at first button changes to orange and then it is urged to select tiles to make meld.
